Overview & Mission

Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship at The Hang Seng University of Hong Kong

Who We Are

Established in 2018, the Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE) is dedicated to nurturing aspiring young entrepreneurs and fostering innovation talent within the HSUHK community.

We empower young people by promoting entrepreneurial spirit, innovative thinking, and creativity through comprehensive experiential learning opportunities.

Our centre serves as a dynamic hub where academic excellence meets real-world application, creating an ecosystem that supports the entire entrepreneurial journey from concept to commercialization.

🎯

Our Mission

CIE cultivates entrepreneurial mindsets across HSUHK through comprehensive training programs, expert mentorship, funding support, dynamic networking events, inspiring guest talks, immersive company visits, and competitive challenges.

We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application by providing students and alumni with practical skills, industry connections, and the confidence to transform innovative ideas into successful ventures.

Our holistic approach encompasses every stage of the entrepreneurial journey — from initial ideation and market validation to prototype development, business model refinement, and scaling strategies.

Message from the Director

DR

Prof. Fu Ho Ying, Jeanne

Director, CIE

"

Welcome to the Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE)! We are thrilled to have you here, where our primary goal is to nurture and empower passionate students and alumni like you, to drive progress and development through innovative and entrepreneurial initiatives. CIE serves as a vibrant hub, bringing together brilliant minds dedicated to advancing humanity through creative solutions and groundbreaking ideas..

At CIE, you will have access to an array of resources, including faculty support, professional mentorship, and training workshops tailored to startups and social innovation. We also offer exciting seed-funding opportunities, co-working spaces, and mixer programmes that foster valuable networking events.

I invite you to join us on this exciting journey of discovery, creation, and impact. Every successful business begins with a single idea, and whether you aspire to be an entrepreneur or are passionate about launching a social enterprise, CIE is here to support you on your journey. Our centre is well-connected with startup circles, financial institutions, government bodies, and professional experts eager to offer their insights and guidance. Since 2018, we have supported more than 11,000 students and alumni in their venture experience.

Remember, every great venture starts small, but with big dreams. Join us at CIE, and you will find a community of like-minded individuals who share your ambitions and drive for innovation. Welcome aboard!

Greeting from the Associate Director

"

As Associate Director of CIE, I am passionate about nurturing the entrepreneurial mindset in our students and faculty. Innovation is not just about technology; it's about finding creative solutions to everyday challenges and making a meaningful difference in people's lives.

Our centre provides a supportive ecosystem where ideas can be tested, refined, and brought to market. We work closely with industry partners, investors, and mentors to ensure our entrepreneurs have access to the resources they need to succeed.

Whether you're a student with a groundbreaking idea or a faculty member looking to commercialize your research, CIE is here to support your entrepreneurial journey.
